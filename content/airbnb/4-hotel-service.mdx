## 2. API design

- `POST /hotels`: Create new hotel
- `GET /hotels/:hotelId`: Get one hotel
- `PUT /hotels/:hotelId`: Update one hotel
- `PUT /hotels/:hotelId/rooms/:roomId`: Update one room of a hotel
